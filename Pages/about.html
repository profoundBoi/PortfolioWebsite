About Me

Hi there! I'm Shafiqa, an aspiring animator, digital artist and mini web developer. I usually spend my time exploring a larger variety of creative spaces. From animation and illustration to theatre and music.

I'm currently in my third year at the University of the Witwatersrand, pursuing a BA in Digital Arts while developing my skills in animation, web design, and interactive media. Outside of lectures, deadlines, and the occasional battle with code, I'm a proud member of SMACK, a cultural collective dedicated to creating opportunities for aspiring South African artists to share their work, connect with other artists, and grow their creative communities.

Welcome to my little art showcase. Feel free to look around!
